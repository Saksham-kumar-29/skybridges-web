Our Aerial Tours

Choose Your Perfect Aerial Adventure

Skyline Manila Tour

Soar above Manila Bay’s dazzling sunsets and take in the sleek skyline of Fort Bonifacio with its cutting-edge architecture. This thrilling tour also includes a pass over Entertainment City, featuring luxury casinos, world-class hotels, and a buzzing nightlife scene for an unforgettable experience.

15 minutes

Metro Manila Grand Tour

Fly above the towering skyscrapers of Fort Bonifacio Global City (BGC) and enjoy the serene green expanse of La Mesa Eco Park in Quezon City. Admire the upscale charm of Makati and the shopping hub of Greenhills, then take in the sweeping views of Ortigas CBD and Manila Bay’s scenic coastline.

30 minutes

Volcano Horizons Tour

Witness the dramatic landscape of the world-famous Taal Volcano, whose 2020 eruption added even more mystique to its iconic silhouette. Glide over the lush scenery of Tagaytay, including the historic People’s Park in the Sky, for breathtaking views and unforgettable memories.

1 hour

About Us

Passion, Adventure, and Purpose in Every Flight

Guided by Passion

At SkyBridges, every tour is a personal journey. Our founder, Saksham Kumar, guides each flight, sharing his passion for aviation, the beauty of the Philippines, and the stories that make every destination special. This personalized narration enriches your experience, making it more than just a tour—it's a connection.

Experience Beyond Travel

Luxury meets adventure in every flight. Our modern fleet of helicopters ensures comfort while soaring over breathtaking landscapes, offering panoramic views and a truly unforgettable adventure. Whether exploring city skylines or natural wonders, every moment in the sky promises something extraordinary.

Profits with Purpose

At SkyBridges, we believe in making a difference. That’s why 100% of our profits go directly to supporting typhoon relief efforts in the Philippines. With each journey, we conduct airdrops of essential supplies and airlift flood victims to safety, helping rebuild communities and provide vital aid where it's needed most.

The Heart of SkyBridges

Meet the Visionary Behind SkyBridges

Saksham Kumar

Founder and CEO

As a pilot in training with a passion for aviation and cultural exploration, Saksham turns every SkyBridges flight into an unforgettable adventure. Inspired by his first awe-inspiring journey over the Philippines, he created SkyBridges to showcase the country’s breathtaking beauty while giving back through impactful disaster relief efforts. With a love for storytelling, history, and the environment, Saksham brings a personal touch to every tour by serving as an in-flight tour guide, offering travelers a deeper connection to the landscapes and communities that make the Philippines extraordinary.
